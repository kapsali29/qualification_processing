Minimum Requirements

6+ years related risk management experience in a discipline relevant to risk management to include banking, insurance, financial services, audit, project management, public accounting or other related operational area AND a Bachelor's degree in Economics, Statistics, Mathematics, Actuarial Sciences or other  discipline OR 4+ additional years of related experience (relevant to risk management ) beyond the minimum required experience in lieu of a degree OR an Advanced degree or designation in a quantitative discipline relevant to risk management and 4+ years relevant risk management work experience OR PhD in a quantitative discipline and 2+ years relevant risk management work experience
*Qualifications may warrant placement in a different job level.*

When you apply for this position, you will be required to answer some initial questions. This will take approximately 5 minutes. Once you begin the questions you will not be able to finish them at a later time and you will not be able to change your responses.

Preferred

Risk management experience in a complex institution and/or highly matrixed environment related to banking, insurance or financial services
Risk management experience in a shared services supporting function (e.g. CFO, IT, Corporate Security, HR, etc.)
Experience documenting processes and/or experience in a control environment such as internal audit, external audit, compliance, regulatory, or consulting
Advanced Excel functions to calculate descriptive statistics for uni and multivariate analysis (correlation, dispersion, etc)
Graphical techniques such as histograms, time series, sensitivity analysis, and regression analysis
Experience in process improvement and development of process documentation such as SIPOC and FMEA
