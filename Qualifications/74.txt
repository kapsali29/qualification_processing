Senior Data Analyst – Master Data Unit 

This is a 6-month contract with potential on being extended based on job performance and business needs. 

JOB DESCRIPTION 

Responsible for data cleansing and migration from SAP 6.0 to new S4 Hana system. Work includes compressing multiple material records with coordination to warehouse management. 

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
Analyze thousands of vendor data records in SAP to determine if any customers in SAP are the same entity based on data elements such as Name, Address, and FEIN.
Data analysis of over 17,000 counterparty records.
Create Mass Upload files to create Business Partner Records in the new SAP Environment (S4 Hana).
Additional work duties related to data migration as needed.
